Get started: Create a goal!

Example, let’s say you want to take better care of your teeth, and to do that, you need to floss daily. Well, you can create a goal called ‘Floss Daily.’ The activity will be ‘flossing,’ and lastly you’ll select a time by which you’ll like to receive a reminder (let’s say 10pm). At 10pm, you’ll get reminder from us on your phone to floss (standard messaging rates may apply)!

Track your progress by logging in and track each time you floss, don’t worry, we’ll take care of the analysis for you and display your data in the current progress tab.
